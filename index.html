MAD LIBS!

A phrasal template word game

HOW TO PLAY:

- First, click the 'GO MAD' button to start.

- Next, choose what story you'd like to turn silly.

- Then, fill in all the prompts with adjectives, nouns, or verbs! (it will specify).

- Lastly, click the 'generate' button to create your very own version of the story!
